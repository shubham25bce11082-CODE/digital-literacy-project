Do’s
1.Think before you post – Consider how your content might be perceived by others, including future employers ,always be careful while posting.
2.Protect your privacy – Use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings regularly.
3.Verify information before sharing – Make sure news or facts are accurate to avoid spreading misinformation.
4.Be respectful and positive – Engage in constructive conversations and avoid offensive language.
5.Build a professional presence – Share achievements, projects, and skills that reflect your goals.


Don’ts
1.Don’t share personal or sensitive information – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or financial details.
2.Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling – It can harm your reputation and escalate conflicts.
3.Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content – This can have long-term academic or career consequences.
4.Don’t overshare your location in real time – It can pose safety risks.
5.Don’t ignore your digital footprint – Remember that what you post online can stay there permanently.
