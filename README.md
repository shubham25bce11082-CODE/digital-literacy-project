As part of this assignment, I took on the role of a **Student Digital Ambassador**. The idea was simple — learn how to navigate the digital world better and help others do the same.

Through this project, I explored:

* How to build a professional online presence
* How to communicate properly in digital environments
* How to use tools for learning and collaboration
* How to stay safe from cyber threats

Each task helped me understand a different side of digital literacy in a practical way.
